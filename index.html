ABOUT ME

I’m Vishwakarma Aniket aka Karmaniket and AV mades, an Engineer at heart and lifelong learner specializing in AI & ML, Computer Vision, Data Engineering, Data Science & Analytics. I thrive in dynamic, data-driven environments, applying technical expertise to solve real-world problems through a hands-on, learn-by-doing approach.
Born in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India, I grew up in a modest background that instilled resilience, adaptability, critical thinking, and meticulous attention to detail. These experiences have shaped me into a determined self-learner who believes that a degree does not define one's profession or potential.
Beyond software, I'm deeply enthusiastic about physics, research, and innovation, driven by curiosity to explore and understand the mysteries of the universe.
I also create YouTube content, where I build end-to-end projects that integrate hardware and software. Open to interesting challenges, collaborations, and opportunities to build something meaningful.
